# Seasonal Produce Guide

Maximize freshness and affordability by eating seasonally! Use this guide to find what's in season in your region.

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Find your location** in the guide below
2. **Identify the current season**
3. **Reference the produce list** for what's peak harvest
4. **Use that produce** in your meal planning
5. **Update location-settings.json** with your region

## Pro Tips for Seasonal Shopping

### Buy in Season = Save Money
- Out-of-season produce is expensive and less flavorful
- In-season produce is abundant, cheap, and delicious
- Frozen seasonal produce is great for off-season use

### Storage Strategies
- **Hardy vegetables** (root crops, squash): Store in cool, dark place for months
- **Berries & delicate fruits**: Freeze immediately for later use
- **Greens**: Use fresh or freeze in portions for smoothies/cooking
- **Tomatoes**: Can, freeze, or make sauce for winter use

### Farmers Market Tips
- Shop early for best selection
- Ask farmers what's peak THIS WEEK
- Buy bulk when abundant and freeze/preserve
- Build relationships with local farmers

### Transition Weeks
- When seasons change, expect some imported produce
- Farmers markets are best for finding first/last crops
- Grocery stores catch on later (pricier)
