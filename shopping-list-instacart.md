## How to Use This List

1. **Check the box** (☐) next to items you need to buy
2. **Click the store link** to add directly to Instacart cart
3. **Or manually search** the item name on Instacart.com
4. **Review quantity** - adjust as needed in Instacart
5. **Verify price** before adding to cart
6. **Complete checkout** on Instacart when ready

## INSTACART ORDERING STEPS

1. **☐ Review this list** - Make sure you have all items you need
2. **☐ Note quantities** - Adjust if needed for your household
3. **☐ Open Instacart.com** - Sign in to your account
4. **☐ Add items to cart** - Click links below or search manually
5. **☐ Verify prices** - Confirm prices match your expectations
6. **☐ Check store selection** - Make sure correct store is selected
7. **☐ Review cart** - Total items and total cost
8. **☐ Add delivery address** - Confirm correct address
9. **☐ Choose delivery time** - Select your preferred window
10. **☐ Review order** - One final check before payment
11. **☐ Complete checkout** - Proceed to payment on Instacart
12. **☐ Track delivery** - Monitor shopper progress in Instacart app
