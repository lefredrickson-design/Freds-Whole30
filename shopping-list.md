## 📝 Shopping Tips

- **Check labels!** Many seemingly compliant products contain sugar, gluten, or other non-Whole30 ingredients
- **Buy organic when possible:** Especially for the "Dirty Dozen" produce items
- **Choose grass-fed beef** when available for better omega-3 ratios
- **Select wild-caught fish** over farmed when possible
- **Buy pasture-raised eggs** for superior nutrition
- **Prep ahead:** Consider buying items that can be batch-cooked
