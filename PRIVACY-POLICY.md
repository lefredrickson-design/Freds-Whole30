## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

**On Your Device (Local Storage):**
- Your meal plans
- Your shopping lists
- Your preferences
- Your health tracking notes
- Instacart address (if you enter it)

**On Our Secure Servers (Encrypted):**
- Your account information (email, username)
- Your password (hashed, never in plain text)
- Your optional backup data
- App usage statistics (anonymized)

**On Instacart's Servers (Not Ours):**
- Payment information
- Instacart order history
- Instacart delivery address
- This data is subject to Instacart's privacy policy

### 4.2 Data Encryption

All data transmission uses industry-standard security:

- **TLS 1.3 Encryption:** All data sent to/from our servers
- **AES-256 Encryption:** Stored sensitive data
- **Bcrypt Hashing:** Passwords (never stored plain text)
- **No hardcoded credentials:** No API keys or secrets in code
- **Regular security audits:** Third-party security testing

### 4.3 Data Retention

**We keep your data for as long as:**
- Your account is active
- You use the App
- Plus 30 days after account deletion (for recovery purposes)

**After that:**
- All personal data is permanently deleted
- No backups are retained
- No way to recover your data after 30 days

**You can request immediate deletion:**
- All data deleted within 24 hours
- No questions asked
- No backups retained
