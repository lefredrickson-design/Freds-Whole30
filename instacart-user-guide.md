# Creating Your Instacart Account

## Step 1: Go to Instacart.com

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to **https://www.instacart.com**
3. You'll see the Instacart homepage with a **"Sign Up"** button in the top right

## Step 2: Enter Your Email

1. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Create a **strong password** (8+ characters, mix of letters/numbers)
4. Click **"Create Account"**

**Password Tips:**
- Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include a special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Avoid using personal information
- Use a password manager if you have one

## Step 3: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox
2. Look for message from Instacart: "Verify your email"
3. Click the **"Verify Email"** link in the email
4. You'll be redirected to Instacart

## Step 4: Add Delivery Address

1. Enter your **street address**
2. Enter your **city** and **state**
3. Enter your **ZIP code**
4. Click **"Confirm Address"**
5. You'll see which stores deliver to your address

**Screenshot Description:**
*Address entry form with fields for Street, City, State, ZIP*

## Step 5: Choose Your Store

1. You'll see a list of stores that deliver to your area
2. **Popular choices for Whole30:**
   - Whole Foods (best Whole30 selection)
   - Kroger (good variety, sales)
   - Trader Joe's (competitive pricing)
   - Sprouts (fresh produce, affordable)

3. Click the store you prefer
4. You'll see delivery fee and estimated delivery time

## Step 6: Add Payment Method

1. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
2. Enter your **credit/debit card** information:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV (3 digits on back of card)
   - Cardholder name
   - Billing ZIP code
3. Click **"Save Card"**

**Security Note:** Instacart uses industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

## Step 7: Complete Your Profile

1. Add your **phone number** (for shopper communication)
2. Add your **name** (how shopper will greet you)
3. Add **delivery instructions** (e.g., "Leave at door if not home")
4. Click **"Save"**

✅ **Your Instacart account is now ready!**

# First-Time Setup

## Configure Your Preferences

### Store Selection

**On your account dashboard:**
1. Click **"Account"** (top right)
2. Click **"Stores"** or **"Your Stores"**
3. Set your **preferred store**
4. Choose **delivery or pickup**

### Delivery Preferences

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **"Delivery Preferences"**
3. Set your **preferred delivery times:**
   - Morning (6am-12pm)
   - Afternoon (12pm-6pm)
   - Evening (6pm-12am)
   - Anytime
4. Add **special instructions** for delivery

### Dietary & Shopping Preferences

1. Click **"Shopping Preferences"** in Account
2. Check **"I eat Whole30"** (if available)
3. Or manually note preferences:
   - "Prefer organic when available"
   - "No grains, legumes, dairy"
   - "Grass-fed beef preferred"
4. Save changes

# Adding Items to Your Cart

## Method 1: Using Direct Links (Easiest)

**From your shopping list:**

1. Open `shopping-list-instacart.md`
2. Find an item with a **[Link to Instacart]** button
3. Click the link
4. **New tab opens** showing that specific product
5. Verify **quantity** is correct
6. Click **"Add to Cart"** (green button)
7. Item is added to your Instacart cart
8. Return to shopping list for next item

**Visual Flow:**
```
Shopping List → Click Link → Product Page → Add to Cart → Success Message
```

## Method 2: Search & Select (If link unavailable)

**On Instacart.com:**

1. Sign into your account
2. At the top, you'll see a **search bar** with your store name
3. Click in the search bar
4. Type the **item name** (e.g., "organic chicken breast")
5. Press **Enter** or click search icon
6. Results will appear
7. Look for the **correct product:**
   - Check brand name
   - Check quantity/size
   - Check price
8. Click the product
9. Enter **quantity** (e.g., 2 for 2 lbs)
10. Click **"Add to Cart"** (green button)

**Search Tips:**
- Be specific: "pasture-raised chicken" not just "chicken"
- Include quantity: "2 lb" helps narrow results
- Check the brand if you have a preference
- Read descriptions to verify Whole30 compliance

## Method 3: Browse by Category (Recommended for Beginners)

**On Instacart.com:**

1. You'll see categories on the left side:
   - Produce
   - Meat & Seafood
   - Pantry
   - etc.

2. Click the category you want
3. Sub-categories appear
4. Click your specific item type (e.g., "Chicken")
5. Results appear - scan through options
6. Click the product you want
7. Verify it matches what you need
8. Click **"Add to Cart"**

**Example flow for Chicken:**
```
Meat & Seafood → Poultry → Chicken Breast → Select Brand → Add to Cart
```

# Managing Your Cart

## View Your Cart

1. At the top right, you'll see a **shopping bag icon** with a number
2. Number = items in your cart
3. Click the shopping bag to open your cart

## Cart Contents

Your cart will show:
- ✅ Item name
- ✅ Quantity
- ✅ Price per item
- ✅ Subtotal for that item
- ✅ Remove button (if you change your mind)

## Modify Quantities

1. In your cart, find the item
2. Next to quantity, click **"+"** to increase
3. Click **"-"** to decrease
4. Click the **X** to remove item entirely
5. Cart subtotal updates automatically

## View Cart Total

1. At the bottom of your cart, you'll see:
   - **Subtotal:** Price of all items
   - **Delivery Fee:** Usually $3.99-9.99
   - **Service Fee:** Percentage of subtotal
   - **Total:** Your final price

**Note:** Prices may vary by store and can change daily

# Checkout Process

## Step 1: Review Your Cart

1. Click the **shopping bag** icon
2. Verify all items are there
3. Check quantities
4. Look for any **"Low Stock"** or **"Out of Stock"** warnings
5. Make any adjustments
6. When satisfied, click **"Proceed to Checkout"**

## Step 2: Confirm Delivery Address

1. You'll see your **delivery address**
2. Verify it's **correct**
3. If wrong, click **"Change Address"** and select correct one
4. Click **"Confirm"**

## Step 3: Choose Delivery Time

1. You'll see available **delivery time slots**
2. Times shown are based on current demand
3. Select your **preferred time:**
   - Specific hour slots (e.g., 10am-11am)
   - Or "As Soon as Possible"
4. Note: Earlier times may have limited slots

**Delivery Fees:**
- Peak hours (usually 11am-3pm): Higher fees
- Off-peak hours: Lower fees
- Overnight/early morning: Lower fees

## Step 4: Review Order Summary

Before checkout, you'll see:
- ✅ Number of items
- ✅ Subtotal
- ✅ Delivery fee
- ✅ Service fee
- ✅ **TOTAL COST**

**This is your last chance to review!**

If total seems high:
- Compare with in-store prices
- Check for available discounts
- Consider different store option
- Remove expensive items if needed

## Step 5: Complete Payment

1. Confirm your **payment method** is correct
2. Check the **card ending in** numbers (verify it's your card)
3. If you want to use a **different card:**
   - Click "Change Payment Method"
   - Select or add new card
4. When ready, click **"Place Order"**
5. You'll see **"Order Confirmed"** message
6. Order confirmation email will arrive in your inbox

✅ **Your order is placed!**

# Tracking Your Order

## Real-Time Order Status

### In Email
1. You'll receive **"Order Confirmed"** email
2. Email includes:
   - Order number
   - Items list
   - Total cost
   - Estimated delivery time
   - Order tracking link

### On Instacart.com
1. Sign into your account
2. Click **"Orders"** in the menu
3. You'll see your **current order**
4. Status will show:
   - 🔵 **Preparing order** - Shopper is shopping
   - 🟡 **On the way** - Shopper is heading to you
   - 🟢 **Arriving soon** - Almost at your location

## Watch Your Shopper Shop (Optional)

1. In your order, you may see **"Track Shopper"**
2. Click to see **live map** of shopper's location
3. See items as they're added to cart (real-time)
4. Shopper can message you with questions

## Message Your Shopper

If items are unavailable, shopper will message you:
- **Example:** "We don't have organic chicken breast. Would you like conventional?"
- You can:
  - Accept the substitute ✅
  - Reject and remove item ❌
  - Choose a different brand/size
- Respond within the app

## Delivery Notifications

1. When shopper is **15 minutes away**, you'll receive notification
2. When order is **delivered**, you'll get notification
3. You can track delivery in **real-time** on map

# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: How much does Instacart cost?

**A:** Instacart has two payment models:

1. **Pay as you go:**
   - Service fee: 5-10% of order
   - Delivery fee: $3.99-9.99 per order
   - No membership required

2. **Instacart+ (Membership):**
   - Annual fee: ~$99/year
   - Unlimited free delivery on $35+ orders
   - Lower service fees
   - Worth it if ordering weekly

### Q: Is Whole30-compliant food more expensive on Instacart?

**A:** Compared to regular groceries, yes. But:
- ✅ Whole Foods prices are comparable to in-store
- ✅ Trader Joe's is often cheaper than shopping in-store
- ✅ Sprouts has competitive prices
- ✅ Saves time = value of convenience
- ✅ Helps you stick to Whole30 (no impulse junk food)

### Q: Can I edit my order after placing it?

**A:** Once shopper starts shopping, no. But:
- You can message shopper before they finish
- Ask them to add/remove items
- Some items may not be available
- Refund issued for unavailable items

### Q: What if an item is out of stock?

**A:** Shopper will:
1. Message you with unavailable item
2. Suggest similar alternative
3. Wait for your approval
4. You can accept, reject, or choose different product
5. If no substitute wanted, item is removed and refunded

### Q: Can I rate my Instacart shopper?

**A:** Yes! After delivery:
1. Go to your order
2. Click **"Rate Shopper"**
3. Rate 1-5 stars
4. Leave comments (optional)
5. Ratings help improve quality

### Q: Do I need Instacart Plus/membership?

**A:** Not required, but consider if:
- ✅ Ordering weekly (membership pays for itself)
- ✅ Want unlimited free delivery
- ✅ Want lower service fees
- ❌ Ordering less than monthly

## Shopping Questions

### Q: How do I find Whole30-compliant items?

**A:** 
1. **Search specifically:** "Grass-fed beef" not just "beef"
2. **Read descriptions:** Check for added sugars/ingredients
3. **Use filters:** Many stores let you filter by "organic"
4. **Check ingredients:** Hover over item to see full ingredient list
5. **Ask shopper:** Message shopper if unsure

### Q: Can I search for multiple items at once?

**A:** No, search one item at a time. But:
- ✅ Use your shopping list (shopping-list-instacart.md)
- ✅ Add items from your list one by one
- ✅ Save your cart (items stay in cart across sessions)
- ✅ Use "Favorites" to save frequently bought items

### Q: How accurate are Instacart prices?

**A:** Prices are usually accurate, but:
- 📊 Prices change daily based on promotions
- 📊 May differ from in-store if Instacart has sales
- 📊 Usually include markup over in-store prices
- 📊 Always check total before checkout

### Q: Can I save a shopping list for next week?

**A:** Yes! Instacart has **"Saved Carts":**
1. After you add items, click **"Save Cart"**
2. Name it (e.g., "Week 1 Whole30")
3. Next week, go to **"Saved Carts"**
4. Click the saved cart
5. It loads all items
6. Make adjustments for next week
7. Checkout

# Troubleshooting

## Order Issues

### Problem: Item is unavailable, no substitutions offered

**Solution:**
1. Accept removal (shopper will refund)
2. Message shopper to look in different section
3. In future orders, use an alternative product
4. Try different store with better availability

### Problem: Delivery is late

**Solution:**
1. Check order status - is it "on the way"?
2. Check shopper's location on map
3. Wait - delays happen (traffic, busy store)
4. If very late (3+ hours), contact Instacart support
5. You may be eligible for refund

### Problem: Items arrived damaged or expired

**Solution:**
1. Take photos of damaged/expired items
2. Go to your order on Instacart
3. Click "Help" or "Report Issue"
4. Upload photos
5. Instacart will refund immediately
6. You keep the items

### Problem: Charged twice for an order

**Solution:**
1. Check "Orders" - are there duplicates?
2. Contact Instacart support immediately
3. Take screenshots of charges
4. Provide order numbers
5. Refund will be issued within 3-5 business days

---

## Payment Issues

### Problem: Payment method declined

**Solution:**
1. Try a different payment method
2. Call your bank - may be fraud block
3. Verify card information is correct
4. Try again with saved card
5. Contact Instacart support if still failing

### Problem: Charged more than expected

**Solution:**
1. Check if you have Instacart+ membership (auto-renews)
2. Check service fee % (5-10% is normal)
3. Check delivery fee (varies by time/location)
4. If overcharged, contact support with receipt

### Problem: Forgot password

**Solution:**
1. Go to Instacart.com
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Click "Forgot Password"

# Tips for Best Pricing

## Save Money on Instacart

### 1. Compare Stores
- Same items vary in price by store
- Check price before adding to cart
- Sprouts often cheaper than Whole Foods
- Trader Joe's usually very competitive

### 2. Use Promotions
- ✅ First-time user: Usually $10-15 off
- ✅ Holiday promotions: Check "Deals" tab
- ✅ Subscribe & Save: Some items discounted
- ✅ Store-specific promos: Kroger sales, etc.

### 3. Avoid Peak Hours
- **Peak:** 11am-3pm (lunch time)
- **Off-peak:** 6am-10am, 3pm-10pm
- **Savings:** Off-peak can be $2-5 cheaper
- **Bonus:** Off-peak usually faster delivery

### 4. Order Larger Quantities (When Smart)
- Bulk items often cheaper per unit
- Example: 3 lb chicken cheaper per lb than 1 lb
- BUT: Only buy what you'll use before expiration
- Freezing extends shelf life for meat

### 5. Buy Seasonal Produce
- In-season produce is cheaper
- Check seasonal-produce-guide.md
- Example: Strawberries $2-3/lb in June, $6/lb in December
- Spring/Summer/Fall cheaper than Winter for produce

### 6. Use Saved Carts Strategically
- ✅ Save your weekly list
- ✅ Modify slightly each week
- ✅ Don't add "extras" (stay focused)
- ✅ Reduces temptation/impulse buys

### 7. Check Unit Prices
- Instacart usually shows "$/lb" or "$/oz"
- Compare unit prices, not total price
- Bigger package often better value
- Example: $12 for 2 lbs = $6/lb vs $8 for 1 lb = $8/lb

### 8. Loyalty Programs
- Kroger: Use loyalty card
- Whole Foods: Use Prime membership discount
- Safeway: Use Club Card
- Trader Joe's: Use email for promotions

### 9. Order Together for Free Delivery
- Instacart+ includes free delivery on $35+ orders
- Combine 2 smaller shops into 1 large one
- Fewer delivery fees = better value
- Plan 2-week shops instead of weekly

### 10. Track Your Spending
- Use the budget tracker in instacart-config.json
- Note prices for comparison
- Find your "budget sweet spot"
- Identify patterns (which items are pricey)
