# 52-Week Seasonal Tracker

Track your Whole30 journey across a full year, noting how your body responds to seasonal changes and seasonal produce.

---

## How to Use This Tracker

1. **Weekly Check-ins** - Complete one per week, organized by season
2. **Monthly Reflections** - Summarize each month's progress
3. **Quarterly Reviews** - Assess bigger picture trends
4. **Annual Summary** - Reflect on your full year and plan next steps

**Track these markers:**
- 🔋 **Energy levels** - Rate 1-10 daily or weekly average
- 😴 **Sleep quality** - Hours slept, quality assessment
- 🍽️ **Digestion** - Any issues or improvements?
- 😊 **Mood** - Any seasonal mood changes?
- 🥬 **Seasonal produce tried** - What new foods did you eat?
- ⭐ **Favorite recipes** - What worked well this season?
- 📊 **Compliance** - Whole30 adherence percentage
- 🎯 **Goals** - What are you focusing on?
